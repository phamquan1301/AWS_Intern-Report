Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1**: [Navigating the AWS Ecosystem: Console, CLI Setup, and EC2 Fundamentals](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2**: [Architecting Cloud Networks: VPCs, Subnets, Routing, and Load Balancing](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3**: [Scaling Compute Resources: Advanced EC2, Storage Volumes, and Cost Management](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4**: [Mastering Object Storage: Amazon S3, Lifecycle Policies, and Disaster Recovery](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5**: [Implementing Cloud Security: IAM, Shared Responsibility, and Identity Management](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6**: [Exploring Managed Databases: Relational, NoSQL, and Data Warehousing Concepts](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7**: [Deep Dive into Advanced Relational Databases on AWS](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8**: [Exploring Data Analytics Services and Data Lake Fundamentals](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9**: [Hands-on Data Engineering and Building Your Data Lake](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10**: [Serverless Analytics and Business Intelligence with QuickSight](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11**: [Introduction to AI/ML Services and Machine Learning Essentials](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12**: [Training, Deploying, and Scaling with Amazon SageMaker](1.12-week12/)
