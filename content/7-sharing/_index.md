### Overall Evaluation

#### 1. Working Environment

The atmosphere at FCJ is both energetic and open. The workspace strikes a healthy balance between professionalism and approachability — team members consistently maintain a positive, collaborative spirit and readily share knowledge regardless of the time. The physical workspace is well-arranged and encourages creative thinking. That said, organizing more regular team-bonding activities could further strengthen interpersonal connections among members.

#### 2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin

The dedication shown by the Mentor and Admin teams was outstanding. Mentors went beyond technical instruction — they actively shaped a problem-solving mindset by encouraging interns to explore potential solutions independently before stepping in with guidance. This approach fostered deeper understanding and longer-lasting knowledge retention. The Admin team maintained close oversight, providing timely support on administrative matters and resources to keep the internship running smoothly from start to finish.

#### 3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major

The internship content aligned well with the foundational knowledge acquired at university, while simultaneously opening doors to technologies and workflows not yet addressed in academic curricula. This made it an invaluable opportunity to consolidate theoretical knowledge and bridge gaps in real-world practical experience.

#### 4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities

Beyond technical growth, the internship offered meaningful development of essential soft skills — including time management, collaborative teamwork, and professional communication in a corporate context. Exposure to standardized methodologies such as Agile/Scrum and structured project management workflows provided practical preparation that will carry forward into future professional roles.

#### 5. Company Culture & Team Spirit

The "Supportive" culture stood out most distinctly. Colleagues interact with mutual respect and a constructive mindset. Even as an intern, I felt that my contributions and perspective were genuinely valued. The team's "work hard, play hard" spirit also played a meaningful role in easing pressure during high-intensity periods.

#### 6. Internship Policies & Benefits

The company's intern support policies are transparent and fair. The stipend is reasonable, and the scheduling flexibility is especially appreciated by students managing academic commitments in parallel. Internal knowledge-sharing and training sessions served as an additional "learning benefit" that enriched the overall internship experience.

---

### Additional Questions

**What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**

The most rewarding aspect was the transformation in my professional mindset. I transitioned from simply executing instructions to actively analyzing challenges and independently seeking solutions. The genuine enthusiasm of the Mentors also played a pivotal role in helping me push through moments of early uncertainty.

**What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**

The program is already in strong shape overall. One suggestion would be to provide a curated reading list or a more structured learning roadmap from the very first week — giving new interns a clear direction for self-directed study before they are fully engaged in project work.

**If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**

Without hesitation — yes. FCJ offers an ideal environment for those who want to experience genuine workplace challenges while still receiving thoughtful, attentive guidance. Beyond skill-building, the program instills the professional habits and attitudes that define a long-term successful career.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations

**Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**

I would recommend introducing regular bi-weekly "1-on-1 Review" sessions between interns and their mentors. More frequent, structured feedback would allow interns to gain clearer visibility into their strengths and areas for improvement, enabling timely adjustments and more intentional growth throughout the program.

**Would you like to continue this program in the future?**

Given the depth of value received, I genuinely hope to have the opportunity to continue — whether through further participation in FCJ programs or, should I meet the company's criteria, as a full-time team member.

---

*A heartfelt thank you to the entire FCJ team for building such a meaningful and memorable experience during my student years. I hope the program continues to grow and welcomes many more generations of passionate, talented interns.*
