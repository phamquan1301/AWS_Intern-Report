#### Areas for Improvement

**Strengthen Discipline and Regulatory Compliance:**
I recognize the importance of taking workplace regulations, schedules, and standard operating procedures more seriously. Building stronger compliance habits will be key to developing a truly professional work ethic over time.

**Deepen Problem-Solving and Analytical Thinking:**
Rather than addressing issues at face value, I need to cultivate the discipline of tracing problems back to their root causes. This approach will allow me to devise more thorough, effective, and sustainable solutions rather than temporary fixes.

**Refine Communication and Interpersonal Skills:**
I aim to communicate with greater tact and emotional sensitivity in day-to-day interactions. Improving my emotional intelligence (EQ) will help me collaborate more harmoniously with colleagues and navigate workplace dynamics more effectively.
